MobiBudget

"Making Every Transaction Count"

Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Kenya face significant financial challenges due to high mobile money transaction fees. These fees accumulate quickly, reducing profits and hindering growth. SMEs often lack the resources and tools to manage and optimize these costs effectively.

MobiBudget

Due to the prevalent use of mobile money in Kenyan businesses, MobiBudget is in development to help SMEs manage and reduce their mobile money transaction costs efficiently.

Services

Cost-Analysis Tools

Tracking, optimisation and analysis of mobile money transaction fees will be offered to SMEs.

Tariff Comparisons

Mobi-budget will compare different mobile money transaction fees of different mobile money operators for you.

Insight Provision

This advisory tool will offer SMES the most cost effective methods of transacting money, increasing profitability.

Financial Resources

Access to educational resources to those new to business will be available to enrich their experience in this field.
