About Us

MobiBudget

Due to the prevalent use of mobile money in Kenyan businesses,
MobiBudget was created to help SMEs manage and reduce their mobile money transaction costs efficiently.

Problem

Mobile money Transaction costs significantly impact SMEs in Kenya.
With high fees and minimal competion, these costs reduce profit and hinder growth.

Services

MobiBudget Services

  • Cost analysis tools
  • Tariff comparisons
  • Data-driven insights for financial optimization
